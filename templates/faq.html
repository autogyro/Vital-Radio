Where will the event take place and how can I register?

You can run anywhere you want and compete with hundreds of runners. You will get real time leaderboard updates to know where you stand. You can register for the event here.

How long is the run?

3 Kilometers or 1.86 miles.

What do I need to participate in the event?

A smartphone with internet data connection and location permissions enabled.

Do all the runners run at the same time?

Yes. The event will start at 7:30 pm (GMT +5) on May 14. There will be an initial warmup period of 5 minutes to get you ready for the run. Immediately afterwards, the application will start to track your run and the leaderboard will be continuously updated.
