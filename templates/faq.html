What does Vital Radio do?

Vital Radio is a non-wearable device that uses wireless signals in the environment to monitor breathing rate.

How does it work?

Vital Radio extracts the raw wireless signals in the environment and tracks their strength. It then applies deep learning techniques to automatically determine a breathing activity window. Afterwards, it extracts features in time and frequency domains to estimate breathing rate.

How much does it cost?

The current implementation is on expensive software defined radios. We estimate a cost of less than 50$ if the radio is developed commerically.
